Data Communication

The goal isn't just to analyze, but to tell a story that expresses the truth about the data while being aware of how it can be manipulated.

Symmetrical (Bell)

Most data points cluster in the middle, tapering off equally to both sides.

Skewed (Tail)

Data has a "tail" on one side, showing extreme values pulling the average.

Bimodal (Camel)

Two distinct humps, suggesting two different groups within your data.
